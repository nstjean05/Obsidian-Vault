## Tables
- Arrange data in rows and columns
- 
```HTML
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
	<head>
		<title> Our First Webpage!</title>
	</head>
	<body>
		<table>
			<tr rowspan=2><th>Names</th></tr>
			<tr><th>First Name</th><th>Last Name</th>
			<tr>Noah<td></td><td>St. Jean</td></tr>
		</table>
	</body>
</html>
```
