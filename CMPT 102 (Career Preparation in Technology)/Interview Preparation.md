### Interview Loop
1. Sourcing (hidden)
	- Recruiter is looking for profiles
2. Phone Screen (Recruiter)
	- Recruiter calls to screen you, check that you're a real person
3. Phone Interview
	- First actual interview, use some technical knowledge
4. Onsite
	- Hands-on work
5. Team Match
	- Sometimes this is included in the onsite interview
6. Hiring Committee (hidden)
	- Leadership evaluates whether you are a good candidate, compared to others
7. Offer Negotiation
	- This is often skipped if you are very new to the field
8. Sign Offer
### Types of Interviews
- **Coding**
	- Data structure and algorithms
		- LeetCode style
		- Problem solving & CS fundamentals
	- Domain Coding
		- Practical application
	1. Phone (1) - Engineer
	2. Onsite (2) - Engineer
- **System Design**
	- Architecture
	1. Onsite (1) - System Engineer
- **Behaviour**
	- Project experiences
	- Culture fit
	- Hiring manager has the largest say in the final decision
	1. Onsite (1) - Hiring Manager
### Expectation and Preparations
- Most important part is getting in through the door
	- Good project portfolio
	- Get a referral
	- Internship (best option)
	- LinkedIn reach out
		- This can go both ways, recruiters to you and you to them
		- How can you stand out?
- Preparations
	- Basic data structure and algorithm, CS fundamentals
	- LeetCode style general practice
		- Binary search, DFS/BFS, Recursion, Stack Heap, DP, ect.

	- Company specific questions
- Expectation
	- 1-2 Coding questions in 1 hour

	- Optimal (runtime/space)
