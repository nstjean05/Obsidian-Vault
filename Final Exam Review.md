## Topic Study Format:
• For each collection (data structure)
– Concepts
– Terminology
– Operations (methods)
– Applying operations to sample data
– Tracing algorithms
– Implementation (array-based & linked list-based)
– Applications
– Order (runtime) (Big O notation)
– Comparison with other collections (data structures)
