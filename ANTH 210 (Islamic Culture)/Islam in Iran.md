## Background
- Iran is a Middle Eastern country, previously known as Persia
- Population ~93 Million
- Speaking farci/persian
### Zoroastrianism
- Ancient religion of Iran, founded by Zoroaster
- Good thoughts, good words, good deeds
- Fire Temple in Yazd
- Was the dominant religion before Islam
### Islam
- Became the dominant religion after the 7th century
- Most Iranians follow Shia Islam
- Small pockets of other religions/traditions throughout the country
### History
- Persepolis was the capital of the Achaemanid Persian Empire in 500 BC
	- King Darius
- Avicenna
	- Physician and Philosopher
	- Author
- Al-Khwarazmi
	- Mathematician/Astronomer
	- Introduced algebra
### Tradition
- Ramadan
	- 9th month of the Muslim calendar
	- Fast from dawn to dusk
- Eid al-Fitr
	- Feast at the end of Ramadan
- Chahar Shanbe Suri
	- Fire-jumping tradition
- Nowruz
	- New year for many Persians/Central Asians
	- Originated in Zoroastrianism, but is not religious, has cultural sense
	- Celebration of family and friends
	- Haft-Seen table is the core tradition
